      Rachael Beneduce

      Rachael Beneduce

      Senior Recruiter @ HAYS USA - HR, Admin & Marketing

      Your new company

      Our client is a well established construction and project services firm specializing in healthcare, federal, commercial, and industrial projects. The company provides design build, construction management, general contracting, and preconstruction services, delivering complex projects for both public and private sector clients. They are looking for an HR Manager to join their team in their Fort. Lauderdale Office!


      Your new role

      As the Human Resources Manager, you will lead all HR functions across the employee lifecycle, including onboarding, employee relations, training, performance management, and retention. Reporting directly to the President, you will serve as a trusted partner to leadership while fostering a positive employee experience and ensuring HR compliance. This is a fully onsite role based in the company's Fort Lauderdale office and includes office management responsibilities such as vendor coordination, office administration, and company events.


      What you will need to succeed

      • 5+ years of Human Resources experience, preferably in a standalone or broad HR Generalist/Manager capacity
      • Experience managing onboarding, employee relations, training, performance management, and HR administration
      • Strong organizational and documentation skills with the ability to maintain structured HR processes and records
      • Comfortable working onsite in a small, growing organization and contributing across multiple functions
      • Strong communication skills with the confidence to provide recommendations and partner with senior leadership
      • Bachelor's degree preferred


      What you will get in return

      You will have the opportunity to join a stable and growing organization that values collaboration, employee development, and work life balance. This role offers competitive compensation, a comprehensive benefits package, and the chance to make a meaningful impact within a close knit team environment. As the company continues to grow, there will be opportunities to take on increased responsibility and contribute to the long-term success of the organization.


      What you will need to do now

      If you're interested in this role, click 'apply now' to forward an up-to-date copy of your CV, or call us now.


      If this job isn't quite right for you but you are looking for a new position, please contact us for a confidential discussion on your career.
