      HR Manager / HR Generalist – Construction Industry / General Contractor

      Fort Lauderdale, Florida

      $80,000-$100,000 + Benefits

      A growing woman-owned construction organization is seeking a strategic and hands-on HR Manager to work closely with the President and leadership team to lead human resources initiatives that support continued growth across project sites in Florida, Georgia, Alabama, South Carolina, and Texas. Currently they sit at about 50 heads but looking to expand with an exciting pipeline of projects in the next 12-24 months.


      This is an exciting opportunity for an HR professional who enjoys partnering with operational leaders, driving employee engagement, strengthening HR processes, and building people strategies that directly contribute to business success.


      As HR Manager you will:

      • Manage day-to-day HR operations across multiple locations, ensuring compliance with company policies and employment legislation.
      • Partner with leaders to support workforce planning, employee relations, performance management, and organizational development.
      • Lead full-cycle recruitment for construction and corporate positions, including Project Managers, Superintendents, Estimators, Project Engineers, Operations Leaders, and support functions.
      • Develop and implement HR policies, procedures, and best practices to support a growing workforce.
      • Oversee onboarding, employee engagement, retention, and performance management initiatives.
      • Support compensation benchmarking, benefits administration, and job architecture.
      • Maintain HR metrics, reporting, and workforce analytics to support business decisions.
      • Ensure compliance with federal, state, and local employment laws and regulations.


      You will ideally have:

      • 2+ years of progressive HR experience, preferably within the construction industry.
      • Strong knowledge of employee relations, performance management, compliance, and HR best practices.
      • Experience supporting recruitment for project-based construction and operational roles.
      • Proven ability to build and improve HR processes in a growing organization.
      • Excellent stakeholder management and business partnering skills.
      • Strong analytical, reporting, and administrative capabilities.
      • Proficiency with Microsoft Office Suite; experience with HRIS platforms and Bluebeam is an advantage.


      This role offers the opportunity to make a significant impact within a growing organization, partnering directly with business leaders to develop people strategies, strengthen organizational capability, and support long-term business growth.


      Interested? Call me to discuss further.


      Call Scott at +1 (656) 202-0744 to learn more.


      Alternatively, send your resume to scott@allegiance-construction.com.
